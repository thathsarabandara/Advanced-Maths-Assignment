Economic Loss Due To Purchsing Alcholic Iteams



In modern times, the success of enterprises is based on not only the strategic operations but also the physical and mental well-being of employees. Because of that most of the leading companies in the world, are mostly concerned about their employees well being to get the maximum output from their employees . This report is mainly going through a monthly economic loss due to use of the alcoholic items by employees in construction sites. In most of the construction sites, the working force such as masons and their supporters are mainly addicted to this alcohol consumption. According to them, they are doing a dangerous job so they use alcohol to reduce their mental stress, however, according to psychiatrists, alcohol is not a way to reduce mental stress. Therefore using alcohol is a cause for economic loss.
